# Project Vision

UKA EximPilot AI is an AI-powered CRM and Business Operating System built specifically for Import & Export Consultancy businesses.

The primary focus is helping exporters and importers obtain Government Schemes, Incentives, and Consultancy Services, with RoDTEP being the core business.

The system is designed to support Marketing, Sales, Customer Relationship Management, Government Consultancy, AI Automation, Business Intelligence, and future B2B Directory services.
